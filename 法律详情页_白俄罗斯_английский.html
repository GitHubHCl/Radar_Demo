Civil Code of the Republic of Belarus (Excerpt)

Part VII Private International Law



Chapter 74 General Provisions


Article 103 Determination of the Applicable Law for Civil Legal Relations Concerning Foreign Affairs

1. The applicable law of civil legal relations involving foreign citizens or legal persons or other foreign-related factors shall be in accordance with the Constitution and this Law of the Republic of Belarus.

Other legal documents and international treaties are determined in accordance with international practices not inconsistent with Belarusian legislation.

2. The parties'choice of law agreements must be explicitly concluded, or they can be directly informed by considering the various conditions of the contract and the actual situation as a whole.

This conclusion.

3. If the applicable law cannot be determined in accordance with the provisions of paragraph 1 of this article, the law that has the closest relationship with the civil law concerning foreign affairs shall apply.

4. The provisions of this Part concerning the determination of the law applicable to the courts shall also be applied by other organs when authorizing decisions on the application of the law.

Identification of Article 104 Legal Concepts

1. In determining the applicable law, if there is no other provision in the legislative document, the court shall interpret the legal concept in accordance with the national law of the forum.

2. If the legal concepts identified are not understood by the law of the country of the forum, or if there are other expressions or different contents in the law of the country of the forum,

If it cannot be determined by the law of the place of court, it can be identified by the law of a foreign country.

Article 1905 Finding out the Contents of Foreign Legal Norms

1. When applying foreign laws, courts or other state organs shall be consistent with the official interpretations, judicial practices and legal doctrines of the countries concerned.

To ascertain the legal norms.

2. In ascertaining the content of foreign legal norms, courts may seek other competent authorities of the Republic of Belarus, subject to the procedures prescribed by the Ministry of Justice.

It includes the assistance and explanation of such organs abroad, or the recourse to relevant experts.

3. The litigants have the right to provide documents that can prove their content with respect to the foreign laws they refer to in filing their claims or refutations.

It is empowered to assist the court in other ways in ascertaining the content of these legal norms.

4. The Republic of Belarus shall apply if it is still impossible to ascertain the content of foreign legal norms within a reasonable period of time even if the measures provided for in this article have been adopted.
