Part VII International Private Law



第七十四章　总则

Chapter 74 General Provisions


第一千零九十三条　涉外民事法律关系准据法的确定

Article 1903 Determination of the applicable law of foreign-related civil legal relations


1．外国公民或法人所参加的、或者具有其他涉外因素的民事法律关系，其准据法依照白俄罗斯共和国宪法、本法、其他法律文件和国际条约，并根据与白俄罗斯立法不相抵触的国际惯例确定。

1. The civil legal relationship in which a foreign citizen or legal person participates or has other foreign-related factors shall be governed by the Constitution of the Republic of Belarus and this Law., other legal documents and international treaties, and are based on international practices that are not incompatible with Belarusian legislation.


2．当事人的法律选择协议须明示缔结，或者通过对合同的各种条件以及实际情况的整体考虑，能够从中直接得知这一结论。

2. The parties' legal choice agreement must be expressly concluded, or through the overall consideration of the various conditions of the contract and the actual situation, the conclusion can be directly known.


3．如果根据本条第1款的规定尚无法确定准据法，则适用与涉外民事法律关系有最密切联系的法律。

3. If the applicable law cannot be determined in accordance with the provisions of paragraph 1 of this article, the law most closely related to the foreign-related civil legal relationship shall apply.


4．对于本编有关确定法院应适用的法律的规定，其他国家机关在授权决定法律适用问题时亦应适用。

4. For the provisions of this Section concerning the determination of the law applicable to the court, other state organs shall also apply when authorizing the decision on the application of the law.


第一千零九十四条　法律概念的识别

The first thousand and ninety-four


1．在确定准据法时，如果立法文件未作其他规定，法院应对法律概念作出与法院地国家法律相一致的解释。

1. In determining the applicable law, if the legislative document does not provide other provisions, the court should make an interpretation of the legal concept consistent with the national law of the court.


2．如果被识别的法律概念不为法院地国家法律所了解，或者在法院地国法律中有另外的表达方式或不同的内容，并且无法依照法院地法律确定，则可以适用外国的法律进行识别。

2. If the recognized legal concept is not known to the national law of the court, or if there is another expression or different content in the law of the court country, And if it cannot be determined in accordance with the law of the court, it can be recognized by foreign laws.


第一千零九十五条　外国法律规范内容的查明

Article 1905 Identification of foreign legal norms


1．在适用外国法律时，法院或其他国家机关应在与各有关国家的官方解释、司法实践和法律学说相一致的情况下对该法律规范进行查明。

1. When applying foreign law, the court or other state agency should be consistent with the official interpretations, judicial practices and legal doctrines of the countries concerned.Identify the legal norms.


2．在查明外国法律规范的内容时，法院在遵守司法部所规定的程序的情况下可以寻求白俄罗斯共和国其他主管机关包括在国外的此种机关的帮助和解释，或者求助于有关专家。

2. In identifying the content of foreign legal norms, the court may seek other competent authorities of the Republic of Belarus in compliance with the procedures prescribed by the Ministry of Justice.

It includes the help and explanation of such an institution abroad, or resorts to relevant experts.


3．诉讼当事人对于他们在提出诉讼请求或反驳意见时所涉及到的外国法律有权提供能够证明其内容的文件，并有权协助法院以其他方式查明这些法律规范的内容。

3. The litigant has the right to provide documents that can prove the contents of the foreign law involved in making a claim or rebutting the opinion, andThe right to assist the court to identify the content of these legal norms in other ways.


4．如果即使采取了本条所规定的措施仍然无法在一段合理期限内查明外国法律规范的内容，则适用白俄罗斯共和国的法律。

4. If the contents of the foreign legal norms cannot be ascertained within a reasonable period of time even if the measures specified in this article are taken, the Republic of Belarus is applicable.
